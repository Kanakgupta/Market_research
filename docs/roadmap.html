Software / SDK priorities

Matter SDK & Certification

Customer roadmap signals (Google, Amazon, Apple, Samsung) show strong push for Matter. Infineon needs to ensure robust Matter support on its tri-radio and Wi-Fi combo parts, including Matter Casting and new device types (cameras, robots).

Horizon: Ongoing (12-18 months)

LE Audio & Auracast Stack Optimization

Broad adoption across consumer audio (Sony, Bose, Sonos) and public venues (Samsung trials). Infineon's BT 5.4 LE Audio support needs to be competitive in performance, power, and feature set (e.g., Auracast broadcast).

Horizon: Ongoing (12-18 months)

Wi-Fi 7 Driver & Stack Development

Critical gap vs competitors like MediaTek, Qualcomm, Synaptics. Need to develop and optimize drivers for Linux, RTOS, and Windows to support Wi-Fi 7 features and ensure interoperability.

Horizon: 18-24 months

UWB Firmware & SDK

Emerging market driven by automotive (Tesla, BMW) and consumer electronics (Google, Apple). Need a robust UWB stack supporting FiRa standards and Aliro digital key profiles.

Horizon: 24-36 months

AI/ML Acceleration on Edge

Customer roadmap signals (e.g., Arlo for on-camera AI) and general industry trend towards edge AI. Explore leveraging AIROC's processing capabilities for low-power ML inference (keyword spotting, sensor fusion).

Horizon: 24-36 months
